# PILOT MANUAL - RunwayHub Community Documents
## Introduction
Welcome to the official handbook of RunwayHub. This guide is designed to help you navigate our community, understand our flight rules, and maximize your experience in the simulation world.

## 1. General Guidelines
- **Community First:** Respect other pilots. Your conduct on Discord and internal networks reflects the pilot image.
- **Safety First:** All flights must proceed within the agreed-upon protocols of the current season.

## 2. Flight Operations (SOPs)
- **Pre-flight:** Ensure all requested logs are submitted.
- **Mid-air:** Report status updates via our dashboard in real-time.
- **Post-flight:** File your flight report (Logbook entry) within 30 minutes of arrival.
